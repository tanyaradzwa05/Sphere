A LITTLE BIT ABOUT SPHERE........

Mr Lebogang Samual Matebele aka DJ Sphere, a dynamic individual with an undeniable passion for artistry and the development thereof. An entrepreneur at heart, Mr Matebele is the co- founder of OTE (Orefile Trading and Enterprise ) and is in partnership with Cosmic–Events and Marketing owned by a young ,gifted and black woman (Faith Edmunds) who has the capacity and skill to market events, conceptualize on events and deliver the end product with benefits to the community and local municipalities . Collectively the duo has to date successfully hosted the DJ SPHERE ANNUAL FESTIVAL for the last 3 consecutive years with the intention of ploughing back into various community based NPOs within the Province and beyond.

It remains undisputed that South Africa is immersed with raw and untapped talent, OTE aims to uncover, nurture and polish the very talent, with an aim to further enhance, support and expose it not only within the local market but within the continental and international centre stage as well..
